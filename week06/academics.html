Wizards

Learn how to bend the powers of the universe to your very whims and pleasures! Our wizards go through a curiculum specifically learning how to use the initate powers of the universe to cause as much chaos and destruction as is possible! Principal Aguefort highly reconds a focus on the study of chronomancy.

Sorcerers

The innate powers you were born with were given to you for a very specific purpose, to end the lives of your enemies! On occasion they may also be used for the purpose of protecting the world from chaos and evil. Mostly for the ending of your enemies! Learn how to do it best within the sorcerers class!

Clerics

Your God has given you very special powers and gifts for the purpose of bringing about their divine will. At Aguefort we say that you do have to do that, because if you don't then you will lose your powers! There is nothing that is preventing you from learning the loopholes of your religion, or even creating your own GOD! (these views are exclusively of the principal Arthur Aguefort)
